Hello! Thank you for visiting our page.

My name is Rumi and I’m a Music and Piano Teacher. I decided to start this series of videos and posts to share with everybody my love for music and share what I have learned through my teaching experience.

I decided to combine all the things that I love: drawing, making music and teaching and that's how Green Cat Piano was born.

My goal is to make everybody enjoy the beautiful and fun journey of learning music and give you these tools for the rest of your and your kids life.

Is dedicated to parents and kids essentially but also to anyone who enjoys games and having fun with music, there is no limit age for learning.
