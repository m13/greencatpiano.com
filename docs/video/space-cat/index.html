This game will help you recognize high pitch and low pitch sounds.

For babies: Show your baby an instrument or toy going up or down according to the sound.

For toddlers and kids: Stand up and raise your hands or bend down according to the sound.

Game Rules

High pitch = UP

Low pitch = DOWN
