This activity is for kids of any age.

If you’re doing it with a baby, you can bounce you baby on your lap following the music or you can try giving him an instrument appropriate for his age, an example could be some shakers, bells or drums.

If you don’t have any instruments, don’t worry! You can make your own, find a small container and fill it with rice, beans or even coins to make different and fun sounds, you can tape the container to make sure is completely sealed and safe for the baby.

You can also use a small bucket as drums.

Use your imagination to create different instruments.

For older kids, you can also use home made instruments and involve them in the creation of them or simply dance and move around with the music.
